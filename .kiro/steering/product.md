# Product Overview

RentalMobil.id is a car rental web application for the Indonesian market. The platform allows users to browse, search, and rent vehicles with a focus on ease of use and local payment methods.

## Core Features

- Car browsing and search with filters (location, dates, brand, type, price)
- Detailed car listings with ratings, specifications, and pricing
- Rental booking and payment processing
- User profile management
- Rental history tracking

## Target Market

Indonesian users looking for car rental services with local payment options (BCA, BNI, BRI, Mandiri, GoPay, OVO, QRIS, ShopeePay).

## Language

All user-facing content is in Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia).
